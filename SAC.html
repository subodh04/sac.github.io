About Student activity council


The purpose of Student Activities Council is to plan and administer activities for the student body, in order to contribute to a more well-rounded social, recreational, and cultural life for the college campus and community. We provide entertaining alternatives to students with our activities and events. The goal of Student Activities Council is to create a fun and exciting experience at ITS IFHE Hyderabad


SAC's various committees encourage student input and participation in planning musical performances, cultural, comedy, leadership and motivational speakers and a variety of other events. Through SAC, students will develop leadership, program management, social responsibility, and interpersonal skills. Since our funds come from student money, SAC makes it a priority to organize events both appealing to students and diverse in nature.
